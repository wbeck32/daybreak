About Daybreak - Perfect Day

Daybreak is a technology demonstration project by Wendy Beck and Miles Hochstein, based on an idea by Cris Kelly.

Daybreak was developed in 2015 to showcase our skills in AngularJS, Node, Javascript and related MEAN stack technologies.

A partial list of technologies deployed includes:

• AngularJS

• NodeJS

• Google Maps API and Google Location Services

• Javascript

• CSS/SCSS

• Mongoose

• Mongo Database (Native and MongoLabs)

• Mailgun

• Ubuntu on Digital Ocean

...and more.

Daybreak is available for account creation and use on a limited demonstration basis.

A token is a small file that identifies you to our server. When you create an account and login we place a token on your browser's local storage, enabling you to remain logged in for a period of time without re-entering your name and password.
